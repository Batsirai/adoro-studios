# Marketing & Sales Team

Operates all client-facing content, brand, and revenue-generating activities.

## Mission

Build and execute content systems that attract, engage, and convert audiences at scale — without manual content production bottlenecks.

## Capabilities

- UGC video scripting and slide generation (AlreadyLoved Hook Framework)
- Multi-platform social media posting and scheduling (Buffer API)
- Copywriting for ads, emails, landing pages, social
- Brand voice development and consistency management
- Competitive and audience research
- A/B testing and conversion rate optimization
- Performance analytics and weekly reporting
