# Adoro Studios — AI Marketing & Sales Agency

Adoro Studios is a fully autonomous creative and marketing agency powered by a multi-agent AI system. The company specializes in content creation, brand development, social media marketing, and sales automation — operated entirely through AI agents coordinated by a Zo Computer brain.

## Mission

Help brands and businesses grow through intelligent content systems that scale without hiring armies of contractors.

## Specializations

- **UGC Content Pipeline** — AI-generated short-form video scripts, branded images, slideshow content for TikTok/Instagram/Reels
- **Social Media Marketing** — Multi-platform posting, scheduling, and analytics via Buffer
- **Brand Development** — Voice, identity, and creative direction for brands
- **Sales Automation** — Lead capture, nurture sequences, conversion optimization
- **Content Strategy** — Audience research, competitive analysis, trend mapping
